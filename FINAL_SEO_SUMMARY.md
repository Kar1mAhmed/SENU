## ğŸ“ Key Concepts Explained

### What is SEO?
Making your website appear in Google search results for relevant keywords.

### What are OG Images?
Preview images that appear when sharing links on social media.

### What is Structured Data?
Code that helps search engines understand your content (services, projects, etc.).

### What is a Sitemap?
A file listing all your pages so search engines can find and index them.

### What are Bots?
Automated programs that crawl websites. Good bots (Googlebot) help you rank. Bad bots waste resources.

### What is Cloudflare?
A global CDN that makes your site faster and more secure.
